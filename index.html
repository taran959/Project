Jawaharlal Nehru

First Prime Minister of India after Independence. Jawaharlal Nehru was cheerful, good communicator, committed, passionate and one who inspired others. Nehru was cheerful in nature who had good company with other leaders of that time such as Mahatma Gandhi and Patel. He had the capability to inspire others and make them do work.
